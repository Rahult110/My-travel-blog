MY TRAVEL BLOG

My name is Rahul and i am professional traveller.
Visual communication is my strength; words don’t always come easily to me.
While I don’t identify myself as a writer I’ve always been a traveler.
From a young age, I was given the opportunity to travel.
I spent countless hours on family road trips & vacations to what seemed, at the time, distant places.
I got my first taste of the tropics in INDOREand learned about ancient civilizations in BHOPAL.
These experiences ingrained themselves on my psyche.
But, somewhere along the way, my life became focused on typical ‘adult’ worries we all know: careers, mortgages and bills leaving no time for travel.
